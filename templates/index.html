in·di·ci·um

(noun) distinguishing marking or sign, symptom

Indicium is a innovative new solution to track the changing of your symptoms over time. This way, your doctor can analize how your symptoms evolved over time. This will help doctors to make more accurate diagnosis. Whenever you are ready to go to the doctor, simply submit your list of symptoms to him/her with one click!
